## Team Roles

Makisala supports two roles for organization members:

### Admin

- Full access to all organization features
- Invite and remove team members
- Manage organization settings and billing
- Create, edit, and delete proposals
- Assign proposals to team members
- Access client management

### Member

- Create and edit proposals
- View assigned proposals
- Access tour templates
- Upload images to the organization library
- View clients

<Note>
  The organization creator is automatically assigned the admin role and cannot be removed from their own organization.
</Note>

## Inviting Team Members

### How to Invite

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the team member's email address
4. Select their role (Admin or Member)
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The invited user will receive an email with an invitation link that expires in **7 days**.

### Invitation Process

**For New Users:**
- Clicking the invitation link prompts them to create a Makisala account
- After signup, they're automatically added to your organization
- No default organization is created for them

**For Existing Users:**
- Clicking the invitation link adds the organization to their account
- They can switch between organizations using the organization selector

<Tip>
  Team invitations are managed through Better Auth's organization plugin, ensuring secure and reliable team collaboration.
</Tip>

## Managing Invitations

### Invitation Statuses

- **Pending**: Invitation sent, awaiting acceptance
- **Accepted**: User has joined the organization
- **Expired**: Invitation link has expired (7 days)
- **Revoked**: Admin canceled the invitation

### Revoking Invitations

You can cancel pending invitations before they're accepted:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Team**
2. Find the pending invitation
3. Click **Revoke**

Revoked invitations cannot be accepted, and the invitation link becomes invalid.

## Removing Team Members

<Warning>
  Only admins can remove team members from the organization.
</Warning>

To remove a member:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Team**
2. Find the member you want to remove
3. Click **Remove** next to their name
4. Confirm the removal

When a member is removed:
- They lose access to the organization immediately
- Proposals they created remain in the organization
- Their assignments are removed from proposals

## Changing Member Roles

Admins can change team member roles:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Team**
2. Find the member
3. Change their role from the dropdown
4. Changes take effect immediately

## Proposal Assignments

Team members can be assigned to specific proposals for collaboration:

- Admins can assign any proposal to any team member
- Assigned members receive notifications about proposal updates
- Multiple team members can be assigned to a single proposal
- Assignment history is tracked (who assigned whom and when)

<Info>
  Learn more about proposal collaboration in the [Proposals](/proposals/overview) documentation.
</Info>

## Multiple Organizations

Users can belong to multiple organizations:

- Switch between organizations using the organization selector in the navigation
- Each organization has its own proposals, clients, and settings
- Your active organization is saved in your session

## Team Limits by Plan

Different subscription plans have different team member limits:

| Starter | Up to 3 |
| Pro | Up to 10 |
| Business | Unlimited |
