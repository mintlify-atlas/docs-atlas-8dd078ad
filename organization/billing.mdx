## Subscription Plans

Makisala offers four subscription tiers to fit your agency's needs. All plans are billed monthly through [Polar.sh](https://polar.sh), our payment partner.

### Free Plan

Perfect for trying out Makisala:

- **Team**: 1 user (owner only)
- **Proposals**: Limited features
- **Tour Templates**: Access to shared templates
- **Storage**: Basic image library
- **Support**: Community support

<Note>
  All new organizations start with a 14-day free trial of Pro features, then automatically revert to the Free plan unless you subscribe.
</Note>

### Starter Plan

Ideal for small agencies:

- **Team**: Up to 3 members
- **Proposals**: Unlimited proposals
- **Tour Templates**: Full template library
- **Branding**: Custom logo and branding
- **Storage**: Expanded image library
- **Support**: Email support

### Pro Plan

Our most popular plan for growing agencies:

- **Team**: Up to 10 members
- **Proposals**: Unlimited proposals with advanced features
- **Client Management**: Full CRM features
- **Collaboration**: Proposal assignments and comments
- **Custom Tours**: Create unlimited custom tours
- **Storage**: Large image library
- **Support**: Priority email support
- **API Access**: REST API for integrations

### Business Plan

For large agencies and tour operators:

- **Team**: Unlimited members
- **Proposals**: Everything in Pro, plus:
- **White-labeling**: Remove Makisala branding
- **Custom Domain**: Use your own domain for proposals
- **Advanced Permissions**: Granular role controls
- **Storage**: Unlimited image library
- **Support**: Dedicated support channel
- **API Access**: Full API with webhooks
- **SLA**: 99.9% uptime guarantee

## Managing Your Subscription

### Subscribing to a Plan

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade** on your desired plan
3. You'll be redirected to Polar.sh checkout
4. Complete payment
5. Return to Makisala - your plan is activated immediately

<Tip>
  All plans are billed monthly. You can change or cancel your subscription at any time.
</Tip>

### Viewing Subscription Details

In **Settings** > **Billing**, you can view:

- Current plan tier
- Billing cycle (monthly)
- Next billing date
- Polar subscription ID
- Trial end date (if applicable)

### Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a higher tier:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade** next to the desired plan
3. Complete checkout on Polar.sh
4. Your plan upgrades immediately
5. You'll be charged the prorated difference

### Downgrading Your Plan

To downgrade to a lower tier:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription** to open the Polar portal
3. Select a lower tier
4. Downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle

<Warning>
  Downgrading may limit access to features. For example, downgrading from Pro to Starter removes team members beyond the 3-member limit.
</Warning>

### Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Select **Cancel Subscription** in the Polar portal
4. Confirm cancellation

After cancellation:
- You retain access until the end of your billing period
- Your organization automatically downgrades to the Free plan
- All data is preserved

## Payment Management

### Payment Methods

Polar.sh supports:
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Bank transfers (for Business plan annual billing)

### Updating Payment Information

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Update payment method in the Polar portal
4. Save changes

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification at your notification email
2. We'll retry the payment automatically
3. After 3 failed attempts, your subscription will be suspended
4. Update your payment method to restore access

## Billing Integration (Polar.sh)

Makisala uses [Polar.sh](https://polar.sh) for secure payment processing:

- **PCI Compliant**: Bank-level security for payment data
- **Automatic Billing**: Subscriptions renew automatically
- **Webhooks**: Real-time subscription updates
- **Customer Portal**: Self-service subscription management

### How It Works

1. When you subscribe, Polar creates a customer account for you
2. Your subscription is linked to your Makisala organization
3. Polar sends webhooks to Makisala when your subscription changes
4. Your plan tier updates automatically based on subscription status

### Subscription Webhooks

Makisala listens for these Polar webhook events:

- `subscription.created` - New subscription started
- `subscription.updated` - Subscription changed (upgrade/downgrade)
- `subscription.canceled` - Subscription canceled by user
- `subscription.revoked` - Access fully revoked after cancellation

<Note>
  The `polarSubscriptionId` field in your organization links your Makisala account to your Polar subscription.
</Note>

Invoices include:
- Billing period

- Amount charged
- Payment method

## Trial Period

### 14-Day Free Trial

All new organizations receive a 14-day trial:

- Full access to Pro features
- No credit card required
- Trial end date shown in **Settings** > **Billing**
- After trial ends, you're downgraded to Free plan

### Trial Expiration

Before your trial ends:

1. You'll receive email reminders at 7 days, 3 days, and 1 day before expiration
2. Choose a subscription plan in **Settings** > **Billing**
3. Subscribe before the trial ends to maintain uninterrupted access

<Tip>
  Subscribe during your trial to avoid any interruption in service. Your trial time is credited toward your first billing cycle.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade immediately or downgrade at the end of your billing cycle.

### What happens if I cancel?

You keep access until the end of your billing period, then downgrade to Free. All your data is preserved.

### Do you offer annual billing?

Annual billing is available for Business plans. Contact our sales team for pricing.

### Is there a refund policy?

We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis. Contact support within 7 days of your charge.

### How does the trial work?

No credit card needed. You get 14 days of Pro features, then automatically downgrade to Free if you don't subscribe.

### What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards via Polar.sh. Business plans can arrange bank transfers for annual billing.
