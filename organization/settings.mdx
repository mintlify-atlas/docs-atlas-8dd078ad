## Accessing Settings

Navigate to **Settings** from the main navigation menu. Organization admins have access to all settings, while members can view certain sections.

## General Settings

### Organization Name

Your organization's display name appears:
- In the organization selector
- On proposals you create
- In team invitation emails

To update your organization name:

1. Go to **Settings** > **General**
2. Update the **Organization Name** field
3. Click **Save Changes**

<Note>
  Changing the organization name does not affect your unique slug or URL.
</Note>

### Organization Slug

The slug is a unique, URL-safe identifier for your organization:

- Automatically generated when the organization is created
- Used in API calls and internal references
- Cannot be changed after creation

## Branding

### Logo

Upload your agency's logo to personalize proposals:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Branding**
2. Click **Upload Logo**
3. Select an image file (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
4. Your logo appears on all proposals

**Logo Guidelines:**
- Recommended size: 200x200px or larger
- Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Transparent backgrounds work best

### About Description

Add a description of your travel agency:

- Appears on proposal cover pages
- Helps clients learn about your company
- Supports plain text (no formatting)

Example:
> "Safari Adventures is a premier travel agency specializing in African safaris and adventure tours. With over 20 years of experience, we create unforgettable journeys across Tanzania, Kenya, and South Africa."

### Payment Terms

Define your payment terms to automatically include in proposals:

- Deposit requirements
- Payment schedules
- Cancellation policies
- Accepted payment methods

Example:
> "50% deposit required upon booking. Balance due 30 days before departure. Payments accepted via bank transfer or credit card. Cancellations within 14 days of departure are non-refundable."

## Notification Settings

### Notification Email

<Warning>
  Setting a notification email is required during onboarding and for receiving important updates.
</Warning>

This email receives notifications for:
- New proposal views by clients
- Team member invitations sent
- Subscription and billing updates
- Important product announcements

To configure:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**
2. Enter your preferred email address
3. Click **Save**

<Tip>
  Use a shared team email (like proposals@youragency.com) so multiple team members can stay informed.
</Tip>

### Email Preferences

Customize which notifications you receive:

- **Proposal Activity**: Client views, comments, and shares
- **Team Updates**: New members, role changes
- **Billing**: Subscription renewals, payment failures
- **Product Updates**: New features and improvements

## Data & Privacy

### Organization Data Export

Admins can export all organization data:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Data**
2. Click **Export Data**
3. Receive a download link via email

Exported data includes:
- All proposals (JSON format)
- Client list (CSV)
- Team members (CSV)
- Organization settings (JSON)

### Delete Organization

<Warning>
  Deleting your organization is permanent and cannot be undone. All proposals, clients, images, and data will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

Only organization admins can delete the organization:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Danger Zone**
2. Click **Delete Organization**
3. Type your organization name to confirm
4. Click **Permanently Delete**

Before deleting:
- Export your data for records
- Cancel any active subscriptions
- Notify team members
- Download important proposals

## Organization Switching

If you belong to multiple organizations:

- Use the organization selector in the navigation bar
- Your active organization is saved in your session
- All data (proposals, clients, tours) is specific to the active organization
- Switching organizations updates your view instantly
