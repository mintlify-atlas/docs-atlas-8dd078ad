## Getting Started

When you first sign up for Makisala, an organization is automatically created for you with a default name based on your user account (e.g., "Your Name's Agency"). This organization serves as your workspace for managing proposals, tours, and team members.

## Initial Setup Flow

After signing up, you'll be guided through an onboarding process to complete your organization setup:

1. **Organization Name** - Customize your agency name
2. **Notification Email** - Set up email notifications for proposals
3. **Tour Access** - Access shared tour templates or create your own

<Note>
  Your organization starts with a **14-day free trial** of all features. After the trial, you can choose a subscription plan that fits your needs.
</Note>

## Organization Details

### Name and Slug

Each organization has:
- **Name**: Your agency's display name (customizable)
- **Slug**: A unique URL-safe identifier automatically generated from your name

### Branding

Customize your organization's appearance:
- **Logo**: Upload your agency logo (displayed on proposals)
- **About Description**: A brief description of your agency
- **Payment Terms**: Define your payment terms to include in proposals

## Organization Ownership

The person who creates the organization is automatically assigned the **admin** role with full permissions. Admins can:

- Invite and remove team members
- Manage organization settings
- Configure billing and subscriptions
- Delete the organization

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization will permanently remove all proposals, clients, and data. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

## Trial Period

All new organizations receive a **14-day free trial** with access to all features:

- Unlimited proposals
- Full team collaboration
- All tour templates
- Image library
- Client management

After the trial ends, you'll need to select a subscription plan to continue using Makisala.
